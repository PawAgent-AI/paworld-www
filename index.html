Frequently asked questions

Which pets does Paworld support? +

We currently cover dogs and cats, with support for other small pets rolling out over time.

How reliable are the AI results? +

We provide reference insights based on visual cues and behavior science, with video evidence clips so you can judge for yourself. For medical concerns, please consult a qualified veterinarian.

Are my photos and videos safe? +

Everything is private by default and only visible to family members you invite. You can choose to publish any individual insight to the Discover feed.

Is it free? +

A generous free tier covers everyday use; extra analysis and additional family seats are available through a subscription.
